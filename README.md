# sudoku-privacy
  Privacy Policy for Sudoku

  Last updated: April 19, 2026

  Sudoku does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app functions entirely on-device. No analytics, tracking, advertising, or third-party SDKs are used.

  Game preferences (difficulty, sound, strict mode) are stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage and are never sent anywhere.
